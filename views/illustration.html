How to play

Memory game randomises a light sequence on the Hue lights for which the player must repeat the sequence to pass each level. As the player progresses through the game each level becomes more difficult, challenging the player to flex their memory capabilities. Follow the steps presented here to figure out how to start and move through the game.

1

Hue Lights

Click the ‘Start Game’ button and watch the light sequence show on the Hue lights. There are a total of 6 lights to watch. As the levels increase, so will the number of lights shown

2

Countdown

After the light sequence has been displayed on the Hue lights, attempt to repeat the same sequence by clicking the corresponding light, in step 3, you’ll see the possible outcomes.

3

Win or Lose

Once you’ve finished putting in the sequence, the game will flash the Hue lights, along with text in the status-board below the light buttons. (Green) = pass, (red) = not passed.
